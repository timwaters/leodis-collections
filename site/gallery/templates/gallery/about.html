Leodis Collections

Leodis Collections is a view into the Leeds Library Information Service photo archive Leodis.net, in showing the changing shape of the City of Leeds. You can view a range of curated photos for a number of collections or themes. The collections are Arcades, Parks, Public Houses, Cinemas and Shopping. Most of the photos have been added to a map and each collection has a map view. Leodis Collections is a pilot website to showcase a range of interesting photos from the Leeds Library Photography Collection of the city of Leeds, West Yorkshire, UK. It is a product of the Leeds City Council's Innovation Labs - collaborations between local technologists and Council departments.

This site is developed and hosted by Tim Waters, a Leeds based freelance web developer in collaboration with Library and Information Service.
